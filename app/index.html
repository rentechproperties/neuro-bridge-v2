    Caregiver Hub

    Daily tips, strategies, and resources to support your neurodivergent or Deaf child at home in Kenya.

    💡 Today's Tip

    Loading...

    Loading today's tip for caregivers...

    📋

    Visual Schedules

    Picture-based daily routines reduce anxiety and build independence for neurodivergent children at home.

    🎯

    Task Chunking

    Break big tasks into 3–5 minute micro-steps with clear visual checkpoints and rewards.

    🌬️

    Calm-Down Kits

    Prepare a sensory box: fidget tools, stress balls, ear defenders, and a comfort item.

    🌟

    Reward Systems

    Token economy with stickers, stamps, or digital badges builds positive behaviour patterns.

    ⏰

    Transition Warnings

    Give 5-minute and 2-minute warnings before changes. Predictability prevents meltdowns.

    🤟

    Learn KSL Together

    Start with 3 signs per week. Learning KSL with your Deaf child builds deep connection and communication.
