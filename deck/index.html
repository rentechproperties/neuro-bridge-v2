Four Barriers No One Else Is Solving

The system failed them
before they opened a single app.

01

🤟 Deaf Exclusion

Without sign language integration, deaf learners are completely cut off from digital education. Most schools cannot afford interpreters. Most apps do not have them. KSL is treated as optional — it is not.

02

🧠 Neurodivergent Invisible

1 in 7 children globally is neurodivergent. ADHD, autism, dyslexia. Zero Kenyan EdTech platforms were built specifically for them. They are given the same rigid formats and expected to keep up.

03

👩‍🏫 Teacher Training Gap

Only 8% of Kenya's teachers have any SNE training. Even when inclusive tools exist, untrained educators cannot use them effectively. Technology without teacher capacity achieves nothing.

04

👨‍👩‍👧 Caregiver Isolation

81% of parents of disabled children have no tools to support learning at home. The school day is 6 hours. Home is 18 hours. Without caregiver support, any platform only reaches half the learner.
