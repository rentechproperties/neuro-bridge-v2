What is NeuroBridge?

NeuroBridge is an inclusive digital learning platform designed for neurodivergent and Deaf learners who are often excluded from traditional education systems.

Different Learning Styles

Visual, structured, and interactive approaches that work for neurodivergent minds including autism, ADHD, and dyslexia.

Communication Needs

KSLintegrated content for Deaf learners and flexible communication tools developed with deaf consultants.

HomeBased Learning

Designed for families and caregivers to support learning at home with visual schedules and progress tracking.

NeuroBridge Testing & Validation

NeuroBridge Africa has undergone earlystage testing through a combination of usability testing, pilot engagement, and user feedback with neurodivergent learners, caregivers, and educators in Kenya.

We have onboarded over 950 early users across these groups, allowing us to observe how different stakeholders interact with the platform in realworld contexts. Testing focused on accessibility, ease of navigation, and engagement with interactive and visual learning content, including Kenyan Sign Language modules.

Through direct observation and feedback, we identified key user needs such as simplified navigation, clearer visual cues, and the importance of offline functionality for lowconnectivity environments. These insights informed iterative improvements to the MVP, including enhanced user interface design and offline access features.

Our testing process is ongoing, with continuous feedback loops from users guiding the development of a more inclusive, accessible, and contextrelevant learning platform.
