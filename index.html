What is NeuroBridge?

An inclusive digital learning platform for neurodivergent and Deaf learners who are often excluded from traditional education systems.

Different Learning Styles

Visual, structured, and interactive approaches that work for neurodivergent minds including autism, ADHD, and dyslexia.

Communication Needs

KSL-integrated content for Deaf learners and flexible communication tools developed with deaf consultants.

Home-Based Learning

Designed for families and caregivers to support learning at home with visual schedules and progress tracking.

NeuroBridge Testing & Validation

NeuroBridge Africa has undergone early-stage testing through usability testing, pilot engagement, and user feedback with neurodivergent learners, caregivers, and educators in Kenya.

We have onboarded over 950 early users across these groups, allowing us to observe how different stakeholders interact with the platform in real-world contexts. Testing focused on accessibility, ease of navigation, and engagement with interactive and visual learning content, including Kenyan Sign Language modules.

Through direct observation and feedback, we identified key user needs such as simplified navigation, clearer visual cues, and the importance of offline functionality for low-connectivity environments.

"Used by caregivers and learners in pilot communities — a parent shared that their child, who struggled in a traditional classroom, was able to engage independently using NeuroBridge for the first time."
